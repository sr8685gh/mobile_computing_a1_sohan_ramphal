Critique 2

The next app that I have chosen to review is called “shopping list”. This app fulfils the basic requirements of a shopping list, but doesn’t have many additional features to it. Some of the flaws are that it doesn’t allow the users to add more than one item of each item. This could cause confusion for forgetful users of this app. This app could be improved by allowing it’s users to make a note of the quantity of items for each individual item.
