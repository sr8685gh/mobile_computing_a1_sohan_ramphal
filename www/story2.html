User Stories 2



Adam is 48 years old and not very good at using his smartphone. The aim of this app would be to make it as easy as possible for him to create and access his shopping list. To do this he will, Type in all the items that he needs and select the quantity using the button widgets Pay for all the items and enter the total money spent.

  • Open The shopping list App
  • Type in all the items that he needs and select the quantity using the button widgets.
  • Enter the total money spent at the store after he has completed his shopping
